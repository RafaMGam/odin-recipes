Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A fantastic festival of cheesy, creamy goodness into your mouth. Let your tongue dance around these warm flavors, as if there were no tomorrow. There might as well not be.

It is said this dish is best served hot, but let it cool down a bit anyway. Make it with love, if possible.

Ingredients

  • One box of elbow macaroni
  • Quarter cup of butter
  • Quarter cup of all purpose flour
  • Half a teaspoon of salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • Two cups of milk
  • Two cups of shredder Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of lightly salted water
  2. Cook elbow macaroni
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  4. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes
  5. Pour in milk slowly
  6. Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes
  7. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
