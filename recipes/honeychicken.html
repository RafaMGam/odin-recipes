Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

Imagine chicken in chunks. Imagine honey. See where we're going?

I've never made this dish before, so caution is advised.

Ingredients

  • Quarter cup of honey
  • Two tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Eighth teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • One and a half teaspoons of olive oil
  • Two skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes
  4. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes
  5. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
