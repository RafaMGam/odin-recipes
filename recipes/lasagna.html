Lasagna

Description

A delicious meat lasagna which is as delicious as it is time consuming to make. It's the perfect dish for a nice family lunch on a sunday, or if you're trying to incite jealousy on the entire neighborhood with its fantastic smell.

Some might say it's the best lasagna in the world, while haters will naturally hate. Be advised.

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • An onion and two cloves of garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with goil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
